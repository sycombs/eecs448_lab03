ABOUT ME


My name is John Adams and I was born in Omaha, Nebraska in 1978. I lived in Nebraska until an unfortunate altercation with a motor vehicle, at which point I moved to San Francisco to be treated for the damage to my hands. It turned out that my hands had been permanently turned into completely sentient robots. Miserable, I decided to get on a flight to England, where I hoped to build a new life. It was not to be. As soon as I stepped off the plane, the people of England laughed at and mocked me, and threw lighted candles. I fled to the opposite end of Europe, where I encountered the one who would change my life. He was a cat with a very special power, but had gone into hiding from the persecution of the anti-cat coalition. He gave me his name and his face. And now I live in peace, under my new identity. Dorbabamon.
